Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival 2019

August 5th-8th, Brooklyn Bridge Park

ABOUT THE FESTIVAL

Brooklyn Bridge Park and Brooklyn Vibes Events Co. are inviting you for the most exciting and exected festival of the year - Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival! This August we will show you carefully selected movies, in different genres for all kinds of movie lovers! Kids will enjoy our festival as much as their parents. We chose some family-friendly movies, intriguing sci-fi, and for those that want to stay with us longer, we have some thrilling and fantastic films to enjoy. We want our guests to experience a unique joy of watching movies open air, under a starry sky.

Our festival is free of charge, but places are limited, so it's necessary to book a seat first. We also recommend taking some blankets with you keep it nice, warm and cozy. We will also have a food court in the area, so you can enjoy the freshest popcorn while watching some great movies we prepared for you. Stop waiting, register and meet the last month of Summer with Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival!
